# Privacy Policy
**SMF Smart Market & Farming App**

Last updated: 23 December 2025

SMF Smart Market & Farming ("SMF", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SMF mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name
- Phone number
- Email address
- User role (buyer / seller / farmer)

### Product & Usage Data
- Product listings uploaded by sellers
- Images uploaded by users
- Transaction-related data
- Location data (used only for logistics and distance calculation)

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device type
- App usage logs (for improving performance)

---

## How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Provide marketplace services
- Match buyers and sellers efficiently
- Process orders and logistics
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Communicate important updates

---

## Data Storage & Security
- Images are stored securely using **Firebase Storage**
- Authentication is handled via **Firebase Authentication**
- Only authenticated users can upload or modify their own data
- We do not sell or share personal data with third parties

---

## Third-Party Services
SMF uses trusted third-party services:
- Firebase (Google LLC)
- Map and location services

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

---

## Children's Privacy
SMF is not intended for users under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Changes will be posted on this page.
